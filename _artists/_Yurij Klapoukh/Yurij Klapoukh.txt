Yurij Klapoukh - Realism

Jurij Klapoukh (born 1963) is a Ukrainian artist. Jurij attended various art schools and art studio in his childhood. After school he graduated from the Kharkov Higher Military Aviation School with gold medal. For five years he served as Soviet Army Officer and took his discharge in 1990. He held a solo exhibition in Kharkov and took part in several group exhibitions.
His paintings can be found in private collections all over the world. He prefers to show harmony and soul in his arts and to be honest towards the audience.
