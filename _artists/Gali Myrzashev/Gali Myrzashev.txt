Gali Myrzashev - Surrealism

The famous Kazakhstan painter Gali Myrzashev had been held in New York under the auspices of Interstate TV and Radio Company "Mir" and Kazakh cultural center. 
A graduate of All-Union State Cinematography Institute, laureate of various international contests represents a series of pictures entitled "Ways of Time". 
According to the visitors the works of the artists are notable for technical skills and deep philosophical conception, humanism based on ethnic roots. 
Gali Myrzashev admits that the fine arts of Kazakhstan claim state's attention and support. Nomadic culture is profound and multifaceted and we have a lot of things to show, the artist said.
