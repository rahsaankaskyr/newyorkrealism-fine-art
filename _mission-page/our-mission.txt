We aim to increase the profile of our members by making sure their work attracts the attention it deserves. New York Realism is also envisioned as a place where like-minded people can develop friendships and relationships to help them build upon the creativity and experience of others. As our membership grows, so does the strength of the bonds we fashion. The goal is an environment for professional advice as well as casually shared creative tips—fertile soil for the seeds of your future success…
